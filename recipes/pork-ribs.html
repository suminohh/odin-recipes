Pork Ribs

Description

Forget about Appleby's. Make your own delicious set of pork ribs from home.

Ingredients

  • 4.5 pounds baby back pork ribs
  • 12 fluid ounces chili sauce
  • 10 ounces plum sauce
  • 0.25 cup soy sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degress C)
  2. Prepare a shallow roasting pan with foil and spray foil with non stick cooking spray. Place ribs on foil and bake uncovered 45 minutes.
  3. While ribs are baking, heat chili sauce, plum sauce and soy sauce in a 1 quart saucepan to boiling; stirring constantly. Set aside.
  4. After ribs have cooked for 45 minutes, brush them with 0.5 cup of the sauce and place back in over and bake until tender; 45-60 minutes. While ribs are baking, brush them 2 or 3 times with the remaining sauce.
