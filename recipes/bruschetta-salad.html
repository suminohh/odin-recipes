Bruschetta Salad

Description

Apparently the salad tastes just like bruschetta but in a bowl. Sounds good to me!

Ingredients

  • 6 Roma tomatoes, sliced into rounds
  • 0.5 pound of mozzarella cheese, cut into bite-size cubes
  • 1 cup of crushed garlic-flavored bagel chips
  • 0.5 cup torn fresh basic leaves
  • 0.5 red onion, chopped
  • 0.25 cup light olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients and toss until evenly combined.
  2. Refigerate until chilled, 15 to 30 minutes.
