Garlic Bread

Description

This is straightforward. Easy peasy.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup butter
  • 1.5 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 loaf Italian bread, cut into 0.5 inch slices
  • 1 package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. In a small suacepan over medium heat, melt butter and mix with garlic powder and dried parsley.
  3. Place Italian bread on a medium baking sheet. Using a basting brush, brush generously with the butter mixture.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven approximately 10 minutes, until lightly toasted. Remove from heat. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese adn any remaining butter mixture. Return to overn approximately 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bread is slightly browned.
